Learning Approach and Strategy

We specialize in providing comprehensive learning approaches for diverse organizations, guiding them through a transformative journey with cutting-edge digital learning solutions. Our services span the entire spectrum, from needs assessment, requirements gathering, and course design to strategy development, implementation, and project analysis. We believe that learning should be creative, engaging, and, above all, authentic. In addition to the above, we offer a variety of eLearning options including

  • Blended learning
  • Digital/e-learning
  • Micro-learning
  • Mobile learning

Blended learning

A blend of in-person and online learning experiences. Combining traditional classroom methods with digital components.

Digital/e-learning

We offer multilingual self-paced e-learning courses, accessible free of charge online and on the Moodle mobile app. The courses allow you to learn about a subject based on your individual needs and interests, and the format allows you to study at your pace, from any location and at any time.

Micro-learning

We offer bite-sized learning content typically range in duration from 1 to 15 minutes focusing on one or two tightly defined learning objectives.

Mobile learning

Our courses are made available for learning on mobile devices (e.g. smartphones, tablets). Enable offline learning by downloading our courses directly from the Moodle app. Save the modules to learn anytime, anywhere, even without internet connection.
